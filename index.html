HOW TO GET TO TAGAYTAY FROM MANILA?

Traveling from Manila to Tagaytay is easy via private car or public transportation. You may take buses bound for Tagaytay, Nasugbu, or Batangas from PITX. Travel time usually takes around 2 to 3 hours depending on traffic conditions. Private vehicles may pass through Aguinaldo Highway or CALAX route for faster travel.
